About Pragati AI for Impact Hackathon 2025

The Pragati AI for Impact Hackathon 2025, presented by The/Nudge Institute in collaboration with Meta and powered by Hack2skill, is a groundbreaking initiative designed to harness the transformative power of open-source AI to address pressing social challenges across India.


By leveraging cutting-edge, open-source technologies such as Generative AI (GenAI), Large Language Models (LLMs), & Data Science, participants will have the opportunity to create affordable, innovative, scalable, and ethical solutions that drive meaningful change and that can be easily adopted by nonprofits who may lack technical, financial & human resources to leverage AI solutions to scale the work & impact.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who can participate in the Pragati Al for Impact Hackathon 2025?

The hackathon is open to working professionals, social entrepreneurs, technologists, and students across India. Participants must be 18 years or older.

Is there a participation fee?

No, there is no cost to participate in the Pragati Al for Impact Hackathon 2025. It is completely free for all eligible participants.

How can connect with mentors during the hackathon?

Participants will have the opportunity to engage with mentors during Phase 2: Prototype Development & Mentorship. Dedicated channels on the hackathon platform will be provided for networking, collaboration, and expert guidance. Access details will be shared exclusively with the shortlisted participants.

Can I participate as an individual, or do I need a team?

You need to form or be part of a team of 2-4 members to participate. Individual registrations are not allowed.

What tools and technologies can we use during the hackathon?

Participants should leverage open-source tools and technologies, especially focusing on Al/ML, GenAl, LLMs, Data Science, and Analytics platforms. The goal is to create affordable, scalable and ethical Al solutions that can be easily adopted by non-profits who may lack technical, financial and human resources.

Are there any prizes for the winning teams?

Yes, the winning teams will receive ₹7,00,000 in cash prizes, personalized mentorship from industry experts, and an invitation to the Pragati Al for Impact convening, offering excellent networking opportunities with Al leaders.

What can finalists expect at the offline grand finale at Meta’s Office in Gurugram?

Shortlisted teams will present their prototypes to an esteemed jury panel at Meta's Office in Gurugram. This high-impact finale offers participants the opportunity to receive expert feedback, engage with Al leaders, and showcase their innovations to key industry stakeholders.
